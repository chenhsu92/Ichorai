Ichorai provides the Industrial AI: the essential support capability enabling “Deep

industrial-grade foundation designed to bridge the gap abstract computation and
reality.

Our initial deployment targets the Healthcare sector, specifically Long-Term Care
(LTC). This system is being developed through our partnership with the Hsiao
Chung-Cheng (HCC) Healthcare Group, ensuring the effectiveness of our Data

After deploying our system, a healthcare organisation’s daily operation is streamlined.
The crisis of navigating multiple systems and manually copying data from
one system to the other is replaced by a single, intelligent dashboard. This directly
cures the pain of administrative overload. Due to the Goldilocks nature of our

client and better after-sales service in the form of software support. All of Ichorai’s
Architecture blueprints put significant emphasis on how human can contribute,

4.2 Phase 2—R&D for Industrial 5.0 (GAE)
We are brainstorming the application of Probabilistic Generative Models to simulate
complex industrial ecosystems. The GAE does not just optimise a single
variable (e.g., efficiency); it solves for a multi-objective function we call Socio-
Industrial Synthesis, where a range of factors concerning the sustainability of the
business in the local environment is considered.
• Input Vectors: The model ingests diverse high-dimensional data, including
local labour demographics, environmental constraints (water table levels, solar
irradiance), and supply chain logistics.
• Simulation Environment: We are researching a “Digital Twin” sandbox where
the AI iterates through thousands of potential factory configurations per second.
• Evolutionary Optimization: The system may use genetic algorithms to “breed”
the optimal facility design, discarding configurations that fail sustainability or
social livelihood metrics, and evolving those that succeed.

4.3.2 Decoupled Value Proposition
The commercial viability of Ichorai is decoupled from the developmental timeline
of the Architecture Optimiser. While the Optimiser is at TRL 1, the Data Manager
(TRL 3) delivers immediate, independent utility by resolving the "Invisible Infrastructure
Crisis" and administrative backlogs for HCC, our launch client. HCC can
see the immediate value from the Data Manager’s ability to normalise fragmented
data signals, which serves as the assurance for early revenue generation. The
Architecture Optimiser functions as an iterative enhancement layer. Essentially,
for the first 2 years Ichorai resembles a project-based consultant, but our work all
leads to PaaS.
